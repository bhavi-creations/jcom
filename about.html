About us

We Provide Most Exclusive Business Opportunities for JCI Members

JCOM is a Business area program exclusively for JCI members. The objective of the program is to develop the Overall Business Growth of JCI Members,through various platforms

Vision


  • To be the Leading Chamber of Commerce of Young JCI Entrepreneurs

Mission


  • To provide Business Development opportunities that empower young Entrepreneurs towards Business Growth.
  • Developing Business Leaders for a Changing World.

How JCOM Works?

4 Weeks 4 Different Types of Table Meetings

Growth Plan & Growth Call Meet - Members will give and seek for business connections to each other as per the need of other members

Solution Board Meet - Any 5 Business Challenges of Members will be discussed and other members as well as solution board will provide solutions to those Challenges

Learning Meet - Audio Based Learning Meeting with Subject Expert/Consultant to sharpen or skills as well as gain Knowledge in the area of Business

Cross Business Meet - It will be Joint meet with Other JCOM Table to explore the Oppourtunity for more Business Connections as well as collaborations

JCOM Board

  • JCOM board will consists of National President , Immediate Past National President, National Director Business, General Legal Council, National Treasurer, Secretary General, one EVP and other members appointed by the National President for guiding JCOM
  • The term of office for the JCOM officers will be one year from January 1st to December 31st
  • The Chairperson of the JCOM board can be appointed by the National President.
  • The Chairman can invite special invitees to JCOM Board meeting in consultation with the National President.
  • The JCOM National Board will meet once in 30 days either virtually or physically to look into the progress and activities of JCOM
  • The annual general body meeting of the JCOM will be held along with the National convention
  • JCOM Zone board will consists of Zone President, Immediate Past Zone President, Zone Director Business, one ZVP and other members appointed by the Zone President for guiding JCOM.
  • The Chairperson of the JCOM board can be appointed by the Zone President. All the table Chairman and the coaches of that particular Zone will be the part of the board.
  • The Chairman can invite special invitees to JCOM Board meeting or to the table meeting in consultation with National President
  • The JCOM Zone Board will meet once in 30 days either virtually or physically to look into the progress and activities of JCOM in that Zone
  • The annual general body meeting of the JCOM Zone will be the JCOM Zone level Business conclave happening once in six months.
  • The term of office for the JCOM Zone will be six months. The period shall be for Jan 1st to June 30th, the next period starts from July 1st to Dec 31st
  • All the JCOM Zone Chairman will be the special invitee for the JCOM meetings
