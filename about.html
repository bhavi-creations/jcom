Jaycees Chamber of Commerce


We Provide Most Exclusive Business Opportunities for JCI Members

The objective of the program is to develop the Overall Business Growth of JCI Members,through various platforms

Vision


  • Leading the way as the premier chamber of commerce for young JCI entrepreneurs.
  • Fostering innovation and collaboration to propel young JCI entrepreneurs towards unprecedented success.

Mission


  • Empowering young entrepreneurs with business development opportunities, cultivating leaders for a dynamic world. give me another point for jcom mission
  • Igniting entrepreneurial spirit through mentorship and resources, shaping visionary leaders for a thriving global landscape.

JCOM Operations

A dynamic 4-week cycle comprising four distinctive table meetings

Business Networking Session - Members proactively exchange and seek business connections tailored to the specific needs of fellow members, fostering mutual growth.

Innovation Exchange Forum - Discussion of any 5 business challenges faced by members, with collaborative input from both members and the Solution Board to formulate effective solutions.

Skill Enhancement Forum - Engage in audio-based learning sessions led by subject experts/consultants, designed to refine skills and expand knowledge in the realm of business.

Collaborative Ventures Forum- Joint meetings with other JCOM tables, creating opportunities for expanded business connections and fostering potential collaborations.

JCOM Board

  • Board Composition: The JCOM board comprises key leaders, including the National President, Immediate Past National President, National Director Business, General Legal Council, National Treasurer, Secretary General, one EVP, and additional members appointed by the National President to offer guidance to JCOM.
  • Term of Office: JCOM officers serve a one-year term, aligning with the calendar year from January 1st to December 31st.
  • Leadership Appointment: The National President holds the authority to appoint the Chairperson of the JCOM board.
  • Special Invitees: The Chairman, in consultation with the National President, has the privilege to extend invitations to special guests for JCOM Board meetings.
  • Board Meetings: The JCOM National Board convenes regularly, meeting once every 30 days, either in a virtual or physical setting. These gatherings are dedicated to assessing the progress and activities of JCOM.
  • Annual General Body Meeting: The annual general body meeting of JCOM coincides with the National convention, providing a comprehensive forum for discussions on organizational matters.
  • Composition: The JCOM Zone Board comprises the Zone President, Immediate Past Zone President, Zone Director Business, one ZVP, and additional members appointed by the Zone President to provide guidance to JCOM within the zone.
  • Leadership Appointment: The Zone President holds the authority to appoint the Chairperson of the JCOM board. All table Chairmen and coaches within the zone become integral members of the board.
  • Leadership Appointment: The Chairman, in consultation with the National President, has the prerogative to invite special attendees to JCOM Board meetings or table meetings.
  • Meeting Dynamics: The JCOM Zone Board convenes monthly, either virtually or physically, to assess the progress and activities of JCOM within the designated zone.
  • Annual General Body Meeting: The JCOM Zone hosts a Business Conclave every six months, serving as the annual general body meeting at the zone level.
  • Term of Office: The term of office for the JCOM Zone spans six months, with periods from Jan 1st to June 30th and July 1st to Dec 31st.
  • Special Invitations: All JCOM Zone Chairmen hold the status of special invitees for JCOM meetings, ensuring comprehensive representation.
  • Formation of Executive Boards: Every JCOM table will establish its own executive board.
  • Composition of the Board Each executive board will consist of dedicated members.
  • Term Duration The tenure of the executive board is set for a duration of 12 months.
  • Operational Period: The operational period for each executive board spans from January 1st to December 31st.
