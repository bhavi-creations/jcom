About Us

JUNIOR CHAMBER INTERNATIONAL


Junior Chamber International (JCI) is an international organization founded in 1915 at St Louis, USA, and having its International Head Quarters at Chesterfield, St. Louis, USA. It is an NGO, imparting training to youth, developing them and transforms them as active citizen, for sustainable positive change around the world. It works with many organizations including UN, for sustainable development globally. JCI is spread in above 100 countries. Each Country is known as a National Organization Member (NOM). In India, JCI was launched in 1949.

JCI India is a registered society under the Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 as amended by the Societies Registration Act (Punjab Amendment Act 1957) as extended to the State of Delhi
