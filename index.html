About Us

Empowering Youth for Global Change


Welcome to Junior Chamber International (JCI), a global catalyst for positive change since 1915. Based in St. Louis, USA, our international headquarters drive our mission to empower and train youth worldwide, collaborating with esteemed organizations like the United Nations in over 100 countries. In India since 1949, JCI operates as a registered society under the Societies Registration Act, extending its presence to the dynamic state of Delhi. Our commitment transcends borders, dedicated to nurturing youth, fostering development, and transforming them into proactive citizens for a sustainable global impact.
